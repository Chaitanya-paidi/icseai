ABOUT PSCMR

About Institution
Potti Sriramulu Chalavadi Mallikarjuna Rao College of Engineering and Technology (PSCMRCET) was started in the year 2008, under the aegis of, more than hundred years old, Sri Kanyaka Parameswari Vissamsetti Venkataratnam Hindu High Schools Committee (SKPVVHHSC – Established in 1906), by a group of philanthropists belonging to Vijayawada. The college is permanently affiliated to JNTUK Kakinada and is approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The institution is accredited with A++ grade (3.68/4) by NAAC and the engineering programs are accredited by NBA. The college offers B. Tech, MBA, MCA and M. Tech programs. Spread over a sprawling campus with aesthetically designed buildings, the college has become the source of inspiration for the students. PSCMRCET has become synonymous with ultra modern learning practices.

PSCMRCET aims to impart quality education supported by societal responsiveness fostering holistic development of its students. The mission of the college is to impart quality and value based technology and management education relevant to contemporary corporate and societal needs through continuous learning, societal responsiveness supported by strategic partnerships with industry and academia. At PSCMRCET education revolves around providing a multidimensional development programme to its students, ameliorating their interpersonal skills and corporate etiquette, thereby resulting in accelerated professional growth. The college aspires to promote leadership through societal responsiveness and excellence in technology and management education.

With a Governing Body consisting of Three Former Vice Chancellors and representatives from I.T. and Core industries – The TCS, Infosys, H-Bots, Eurth Techtronics, Galaxe Solutions Inc., Efftronics, Govt. Water Resource Dept., Athena Power, Price Water, Sesha Sai Knittings, Minerva Group of Hotels – PSCMRCET continues to be rated as one of the leading technical colleges in the capital region of Andhra Pradesh. The academic programs offered in both Engineering and Management are highly regarded. The institute is known for imparting high-quality professional education and training in an atmosphere of discipline. All the stakeholders work towards fulfilling this mission, shaping our students into people who can play an effective role in the development of a knowledge society.

The college has been committed to the vision of making a significant contribution to a highly advanced technological society with profound human values by sending forth students as people of unparalleled expertise and a high sense of ethical values.

Beyond curriculum, topics on niche technologies are addressed by inviting expert resource persons from industry and academia. The A.P. State Skill Development Council has conducted more than 151 training programs in the last three years. The college’s commitment to providing internships is evident from the award it received from AICTE for the maximum number of internships in the southern region through Internshala.

With an ardent commitment to help students become perfect wholesome personalities, the College envisages a campus that promotes healthy interaction between faculty and students and among the students. A well-structured student counseling system promotes team spirit and cooperative learning. Extra and co-curricular activities are encouraged through various campus clubs and committees, such as the PSCMR Toastmasters Club, Chess Club, Roteract Club, and Music Club, along with student chapters of esteemed professional bodies like the Institution of Engineers, Computer Society of India, and Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers. Student participation in project expos, hackathons, research paper submissions, and sports at inter and intra-college levels is highly appreciated and encouraged, with students being reimbursed for travel expenses and registration fees.

Teaching staff are sponsored to attend FDPs and seminars, while non-teaching staff are sent to skill-upgradation programs with financial aid. All campus activities are meticulously planned and executed by students under the mentorship of experienced faculty.

PSCMR College of Engineering and Technology has become synonymous with ultra-modern learning and excellent placements. Within a short span, the college has emerged as one of the prestigious educational institutions in the region, attracting students from across the state. The fact that more than 67% of our students are girls is a testament to the faith and trust of parents in the institution’s strict, no-compromise attitude toward discipline.

Preliminary Chapter Titles and Detailed Table of Contents

  1. Data Science and Computational Business Systems Overview: This chapter sets the stage for the book by exploring the key concepts and significance of data science and computational systems in business contexts. It will discuss the evolution of these fields, their impact on various industries, and provide an overview of the methodologies and tools commonly used. The chapter will also outline the goals and structure of the book.
  2. Data Collection and Management: This chapter delves into the techniques and technologies for gathering and managing data. It covers various data sources, methods for data acquisition, and best practices for ensuring data quality and integrity. Topics include data warehousing, data lakes, and data integration strategies.
  3. Data Preprocessing and Cleaning: Here, the focus will be on the essential steps of data preprocessing and cleaning, which are crucial for accurate analysis. The chapter will address methods for handling missing data, data normalization, transformation, and error detection. It will also highlight tools and techniques for automating these processes.
  4. Exploratory Data Analysis Techniques: This chapter explores techniques for exploring and analyzing data to uncover patterns, trends, and insights. It includes methods such as statistical summaries, data visualization, and correlation analysis. Practical examples and case studies will illustrate how exploratory data analysis can guide decision-making.
  5. Machine Learning Models: This chapter provides an overview of machine learning models used in data science. It covers supervised learning techniques such as regression and classification, as well as unsupervised learning techniques like clustering and dimensionality reduction. Key algorithms and their applications in business contexts will be discussed.
  6. Advanced Machine Learning Techniques: Building on the previous chapter, this section explores more complex machine learning techniques including deep learning, reinforcement learning, and ensemble methods. The chapter will detail the architecture of neural networks, the application of algorithms to large datasets, and the challenges associated with advanced models.
  7. Big Data Frameworks and Platforms: This chapter examines the infrastructure and tools necessary for managing and processing large volumes of data. Topics include Hadoop, Apache Spark, and other big data frameworks. The chapter will also address scalability issues, distributed computing, and cloud-based solutions.
  8. Real-Time Data Processing: Focusing on the importance and techniques of processing data in real-time, this chapter will cover stream processing frameworks, real-time analytics tools, and their applications in various industries. It will also discuss the challenges of latency and throughput.
  9. Data Visualization Methods: This chapter covers the principles and tools of data visualization, emphasizing the creation of effective charts, graphs, and interactive dashboards. It will explore various visualization techniques and discuss how to present data in a clear and actionable way. Case studies will illustrate successful visualization practices.
  10. Business Intelligence and Analytics: The focus here is on the use of data science techniques to drive business intelligence and analytics. The chapter will cover business intelligence platforms, data mining techniques, and how analytics can inform strategic business decisions. Practical examples from different sectors will be included.
  11. Ethical Considerations in Data Science: This chapter addresses the ethical issues associated with data science practices, including data privacy, security, and fairness. It will discuss the implications of data misuse and provide guidelines for ethical data handling. Relevant case studies will highlight both positive and negative outcomes.
  12. Regulatory and Compliance Issues: This section will explore the legal and regulatory frameworks governing data usage and management. Topics include GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant regulations. The chapter will also cover compliance strategies and the impact of regulations on business operations.
  13. Applications of Data Science in Various Industries: This chapter provides an overview of how data science is applied across different industries, such as finance, healthcare, retail, and manufacturing. It will include case studies and examples of successful data-driven projects in each sector.
  14. Case Studies in Computational Business Systems: Featuring detailed case studies, this chapter will illustrate real-world applications of computational business systems. Each case study will examine the problem, the data science approach used, and the outcomes. Lessons learned and best practices will be highlighted.
  15. Emerging Trends in Data Science and Computational Business Systems: The final chapter will look forward to emerging trends and future directions in the field. Topics will include advancements in artificial intelligence, quantum computing, and their potential impact on data science and business systems. The chapter will provide insights into upcoming innovations and their implications.

Chapter by Chapter Synopsis

  1. Data Science and Computational Business Systems Overview: This chapter introduces the foundational concepts of data science and its integration with computational business systems. It provides a historical perspective on the evolution of these fields and outlines their significance in modern business environments. The chapter also highlights key trends and sets the context for the book’s exploration of advanced topics.
  2. Data Collection and Management: This chapter focuses on methods for acquiring and managing data effectively. It covers various data sources, including internal and external datasets, and introduces techniques for data acquisition such as web scraping and API integration. The chapter also discusses data storage solutions like databases and data lakes, emphasizing strategies for maintaining data integrity and quality.
  3. Data Preprocessing and Cleaning: Essential for preparing data for analysis, this chapter delves into data preprocessing and cleaning techniques. It addresses methods for handling missing data, outliers, and inconsistencies. The chapter provides practical guidelines for data normalization, transformation, and feature engineering, and discusses tools for automating these processes.
  4. Exploratory Data Analysis Techniques: This chapter covers exploratory data analysis (EDA) techniques used to understand data characteristics and uncover patterns. It includes statistical summaries, graphical representations, and correlation analysis. Practical examples demonstrate how EDA can inform further analysis and hypothesis generation.
  5. Machine Learning Models: This chapter introduces machine learning models essential for data science applications. It covers supervised learning techniques such as linear regression and classification, as well as unsupervised learning methods like clustering and dimensionality reduction. The chapter includes practical applications and examples of these models in business settings.
  6. Advanced Machine Learning Techniques: Building on earlier discussions, this chapter explores advanced machine learning techniques including deep learning, reinforcement learning, and ensemble methods. It explains neural network architectures, advanced algorithms, and their applications. The chapter also addresses the challenges and innovations in these advanced areas.
  7. Big Data Frameworks and Platforms: This chapter examines the frameworks and platforms designed for managing and processing large datasets. It covers technologies such as Hadoop and Apache Spark, discussing their architecture, capabilities, and use cases. The chapter also addresses scalability issues and the role of cloud computing in big data processing.
  8. Real-Time Data Processing: Focused on the processing of data as it is generated, this chapter explores real-time data processing technologies and frameworks. It includes discussions on stream processing with tools like Apache Kafka and Apache Flink, and the challenges of achieving low latency and high throughput in real-time systems.
  9. Data Visualization Methods: This chapter covers techniques and best practices for visualizing data effectively. It includes discussions on creating informative charts, graphs, and interactive dashboards. The chapter also highlights tools and software for data visualization, and offers guidelines for presenting data in a way that supports decision-making.
  10. Business Intelligence and Analytics: This chapter explores how data science techniques are used to drive business intelligence and analytics. It covers business intelligence platforms, data mining techniques, and the role of analytics in strategic business decisions. The chapter includes case studies demonstrating how analytics can enhance business performance.
  11. Ethical Considerations in Data Science: This chapter addresses the ethical issues associated with data science practices. It covers data privacy, security, and fairness, and provides guidelines for responsible data use. The chapter also discusses the consequences of unethical data practices through relevant case studies.
  12. Regulatory and Compliance Issues: This chapter explores the legal and regulatory frameworks governing data management. It includes discussions on regulations such as GDPR and CCPA, and their impact on data practices. The chapter provides strategies for achieving compliance and highlights the importance of adhering to legal requirements.

ABOUT DATA SCIENCE

With the advent of IoT (Internet of Things) and Industry 4.0, the volume of data is growing at an exponential rate. Given the massive amount of data that is produced, it has led to the matured discipline of Data Science that involves collection, visualization, processing, and modeling of large and complex data sets from different domains and sources.

Data science is an essential part of many industries today, and its popularity has grown over the years. Companies have started implementing data science techniques to grow their business and increase customer satisfaction. Its immense popularity has increased the demanding interest among students to choose it as a career option.

Data Science is an emerging area of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). The Department of CSE-Data Science was established in the year 2022 with an annual intake of 60 students. The P.G program in Data science was established in the year 2022 with an annual intake of 18 students.

BTech CSE (Data Science) is a 4-year undergraduate level course designed for you to build a distinctive career in analytical and leadership roles in various sectors. The course is new and trending, with a perfect blend of machine learning, big data analytics, and statistics, helping students gain experience in solving problems with real-world data.

This course also gives importance to areas like big data analytics, fuzzy technologies, and artificial neural networks. It helps students in getting ready with skills to perform decisions that are based on data analysis. Throughout the duration of the programme, the students are taught how to amalgamate business knowledge, tools, and statistics to generate business value in creative ways.
